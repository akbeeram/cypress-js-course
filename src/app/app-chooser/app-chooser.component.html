Social Media App

  • Login to the app and select Social Media App and verify for the url.
  • Left side Navigation, Verify that

    • there are 250 posts, 2.5K Followers and 5K Following for the user.
    • there are 7 links on the left side navigation and 6 links beneath the navigation links.
    • 5 posts are shown in the Recent Posts section for the user.
    • once select a different number of posts to show, the same number of posts are displayed.
  • Middle Section, Verify that

    • each post has 4 different action icons along with count
    • when clicked on comment, atleast 5 comments for the post are displayed and are hidden when we click it again.
    • when clicked on heart, the count increases and color changes to reflect like and the reverse happens when we click on it again
    • when clicked on share icon, the count increases by as many clicks.
    • when clicked on save, the count increases and color changes to reflect save and the reverse happens when we click on it again
    • when comments section is open for any post:

      • when clicked on heart, the count increases and color changes to reflect like and the reverse happens when we click on it again
      • when clicked on share icon, the count increases by as many clicks.
      • when clicked on save, the count increases and color changes to reflect save and the reverse happens when we click on it again
  • Right Side Section, Verify that:

    • two sections are displayed, namely Who to follow and Today's news
    • In Today's news, atleast 4 news items are displayed
    • In Who to follow, Verify that:

      • user has atleast 3 friends
      • when click on plus, the person is added as a friend to the user and on click again, is removed
